# Marketing Video Prompt for Veo 3

## Primary Video Prompt (60-second hero video)

**Scene-by-scene breakdown for AI video generation:**

### Opening Scene (0-5 seconds)
"Cinematic shot: A golden retriever sitting in a sunlit garden, camera slowly zooms in on the dog's face. Warm, natural lighting. The image freezes and transforms with magical golden particles into a classical oil painting with visible brushstrokes, hanging in an elegant gold frame on a living room wall."

### Transformation Montage (5-20 seconds)
"Split-screen transformation sequence: On the left, original photos sliding up - a child's portrait, a wedding couple, a landscape sunset, a cat close-up. On the right, each photo transforms with swirling paint effects into oil paintings with thick impasto textures. Golden particles and paint splashes transition between each transformation. Smooth, satisfying animations."

### App Interface Demo (20-35 seconds)
"Clean, modern smartphone screen recording: User uploads a family photo, selects 'Classic Oil' style, watches a progress bar with paint brush animation. The result appears with a satisfying 'pop' effect. User swipes through different styles: Impressionist (soft, dreamy), Modern (bold strokes), Classic (Rembrandt-style). Each swipe shows smooth transitions. UI is minimal, elegant with amber accents."

### Happy Customers (35-45 seconds)
"Montage of diverse people receiving printed oil paintings: Young woman unwrapping a canvas print of her dog, elderly couple hanging their wedding photo transformation above fireplace, artist admiring their self-portrait in oil style. Each person shows genuine delight. Warm, homey environments. Natural reactions and smiles."

### Product Showcase (45-55 seconds)
"Elegant product shots: Canvas prints in various sizes displayed on white pedestals, rotating slowly. Close-up of canvas texture showing paint-like surface. Gallery wall with multiple oil painting prints. Premium packaging being opened. Soft studio lighting, minimalist aesthetic."

### Call to Action (55-60 seconds)
"Logo animation with paint splash effect forming 'OilPaintingApp'. Text overlay: 'Transform Your Photos Today'. Website URL fades in. Background: time-lapse of blank canvas being painted with swirling colors. Uplifting music crescendo."

## Alternative Shorter Versions

### 15-Second Social Media Version
"Quick cuts: Photo of dog → paint swirl transition → oil painting. Photo of couple → paint effect → framed artwork. App interface showing '3 simple steps'. Multiple canvas prints displayed. Logo with 'Transform Photos to Art in Seconds'. Energetic, fast-paced."

### 30-Second Instagram/TikTok Version
"Hook: 'Turn any photo into a masterpiece!' Fast transformation of influencer selfie to oil painting. App demo on phone - upload, select style, done! Before/after gallery showing 5 transformations. Customer unboxing beautiful canvas print. CTA: 'Link in bio - 50% off today!'"

## Visual Style Guidelines

- **Color Palette**: Warm amber, gold, cream, rich browns (oil paint colors)
- **Transitions**: Paint splash, brush stroke wipes, canvas texture overlays
- **Music**: Uplifting orchestral or modern classical, building to inspirational crescendo
- **Text**: Elegant serif fonts for headers, clean sans-serif for UI
- **Lighting**: Warm, museum-quality lighting for artwork shots
- **Effects**: Particle effects should look like paint droplets, golden dust

## Key Messages to Convey

1. **Instant Transformation**: Photos become art in seconds
2. **Professional Quality**: Museum-worthy results
3. **Easy Process**: 3 simple steps
4. **Emotional Value**: Preserve memories as timeless art
5. **Gift Potential**: Perfect for special occasions
6. **Affordability**: Premium art made accessible

## Technical Specifications

- **Resolution**: 4K (3840 x 2160) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 60fps for smooth animations
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9 (main), 9:16 (stories), 1:1 (feed posts)
- **Duration Options**: 60s (full), 30s (ads), 15s (social), 6s (bumper)
