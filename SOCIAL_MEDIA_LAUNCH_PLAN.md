# Social Media Launch Plan: AI Oil Painting Conversion App

## Executive Summary
Comprehensive social media strategy for launching an AI-powered oil painting conversion app, targeting art lovers, gift buyers, pet owners, and families.

## 1. Pre-Launch Strategy (2 Weeks Before Launch)

### Week 1: Build Anticipation
- **Teaser Content**
  - Post blurred transformations with "Coming Soon" overlays
  - Share time-lapse videos of AI creating paintings
  - Behind-the-scenes development footage
  - Countdown posts with daily reveals

- **Early Access Program**
  - Recruit 50 beta testers from social media
  - Create exclusive Facebook group for testers
  - Generate initial testimonials and UGC

### Week 2: Influencer Soft Launch
- **Micro-Influencer Partnerships** (10k-50k followers)
  - Pet photographers/influencers
  - Home decor bloggers
  - Art enthusiasts
  - Family lifestyle creators
  - Provide early access + free canvas print

- **Content Creation**
  - Influencers create "first look" content
  - Unboxing videos of canvas prints
  - Before/after transformation posts
  - Stories showing the app process

## 2. Launch Week Activities

### Day 1: Grand Launch
- **9 AM EST**: Coordinated posts across all platforms
- **12 PM EST**: Instagram Live demo
- **3 PM EST**: TikTok challenge announcement
- **6 PM EST**: Facebook Live Q&A

### Daily Themes
- **Monday**: Launch announcement + 48-hour discount (25% off)
- **Tuesday**: Pet Portrait Tuesday (#PetsToPaintings)
- **Wednesday**: Wedding Wednesday (couples' photos)
- **Thursday**: Throwback Thursday (vintage photo transformations)
- **Friday**: Feature Friday (showcase three styles)
- **Weekend**: User spotlight + contest announcement

## 11. Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses
- **Technical glitches**: Immediate acknowledgment, timeline for fix
- **Quality concerns**: Highlight money-back guarantee, share positive reviews
- **AI ethics questions**: Educational content about AI assistance, not replacement
- **Pricing complaints**: Emphasize quality, comparison with traditional portraits
