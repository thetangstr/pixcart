KEY LEARNINGS & INSIGHTS
----------------------------------------

1. CONTROLNET WEIGHTS ARE CRITICAL
   • Canny: 0.6-0.7 optimal for subject preservation
   • Depth: 0.3-0.4 maintains dimensionality without over-constraining
   • Higher weights preserve geometry but reduce artistic transformation

2. DENOISING STRENGTH SWEET SPOTS
   • Classic Portrait: 0.40-0.45 (balanced transformation)
   • Thick & Textured: 0.50-0.60 (allows for bold strokes)
   • Soft & Dreamy: 0.35-0.40 (gentle transformation)

3. PROMPT ENGINEERING DISCOVERIES
   • Specific technique keywords ('impasto', 'glazing') > generic terms
   • Negative prompts crucial for avoiding digital/photo artifacts
   • Style-specific vocabulary improves authenticity significantly

4. CFG SCALE OPTIMIZATION
   • Lower CFG (6-7) for soft, impressionist styles
   • Medium CFG (7-8) for classic, balanced results
   • Higher CFG (8-9) for bold, textured styles
